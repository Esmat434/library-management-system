Connecting Our Community Through Knowledge

For over 50 years, our library has been a cornerstone of the community, fostering a love for reading and lifelong learning.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide free and equal access to information, knowledge, and entertainment. We are dedicated to empowering our community by fostering literacy, supporting education, and encouraging a passion for discovery in a welcoming and inclusive environment.

Support Our Mission

Our Journey

A brief history of our library's milestones.

1974

The library first opens its doors in the old town hall, with a collection of just 500 books donated by the community.

1995

We move to our current, purpose-built location, expanding our collection and introducing public access computers.

2010

The digital era begins with the launch of our first e-book and audiobook lending service.
